MARGIN

Let's take a look at margins, ok?

Margins make an invisible square around a div :)

They work like this:
"margin:top/bottom left/right"

I can also make each margin different.
They work in clockwise direction:
top, right, bottom, left.

Also, "Margin-DIRECTION" add a margin in only one direction.

Example: The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog. CSS is awesome!
